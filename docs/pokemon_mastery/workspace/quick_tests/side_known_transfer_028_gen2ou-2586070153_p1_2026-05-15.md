## Reusable Lessons

1. AgilityPass answer is not always phaze first. When the phazer is low or the
   receiver is not yet known, Reflect can be the converter that lets the next
   answer survive.
2. Against Jolteon, Encore only beats setup if the target stays. If Baton Pass
   happens first, the receiver map matters more than the current Jolteon.
3. Starmie Rapid Spin can outrank exact Psychic damage into low Cloyster when
   Spikes are already on our side and the switch branch is likely.
4. In GSC logs, residual poison after the faster move can KO the target before
   the slower Pokemon's selected move is logged. Do not count a slow support
   spend after the target is already dying to residual.
