## Policy Extraction

Trigger:
  The named branch is not just "their receiver" but a move-class punish such as
  DynamicPunch, Explosion absorption, or low-piece cash-out.

Default:
  Choose the action class that beats the move-class punish: immunity,
  absorber, counter-switch, coverage, or cash-out.

Exceptions:
  Keep active damage when it still changes the receiver's route more than the
  handoff, or when the supposed absorber has no safe entry.

Worst branch:
  You name the punish, then choose a move that only answers the Pokemon
  currently visible and hand the opponent either a free immunity or a resisted
  cash-out.
