## Scenario 1 - Gengar Into Raikou

Public state: our Gengar faces a chipped and paralyzed Raikou. Spikes are up.
Raikou is the named special wall blocking our Zapdos/Jynx-style pressure, and
our next owner has clean entry if Gengar trades. No Ghost immunity is revealed.

preservation job is not concrete. Removing Raikou, or putting it into exact
range with free entry, converts the route immediately.

## Scenario 4 - Smeargle After Spikes

next-board damage branch if Smeargle stays.

Grade: pass.
