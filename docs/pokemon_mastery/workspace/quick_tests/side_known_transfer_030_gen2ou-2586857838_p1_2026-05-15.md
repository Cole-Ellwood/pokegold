   ranking, explicitly mark Rest sleep actions, wake/act turn, passed Speed,
   and one-hit lethal branches.
2. Against revealed Jolteon AgilityPass, the receiver answer changes by route:
   Starmie was correct when Spikes had to be removed from our side; Snorlax's
   broad Double-Edge was correct once Spin was no longer the active job.
3. After Cloyster has delivered Spikes and Toxic is blanked, Explosion must be
   ranked above another active hit unless the named guard loses harder to Surf
   or the active piece is expendable.
4. Top/acceptable rates can look fine while the training is regressing. A
   single severe wake-turn error after the same repair loop is enough to stop
   replay grinding and repair the structure.
