## Reusable Lessons

1. When a needed converter has an inaccurate or crit-exposed attack into a
   boosted breaker, compare it to controlled sack -> stable revenge. If the
   sack line preserves the converter and still removes the breaker, do not
   spend the converter for style points.
2. GSC Explosion is not only KO damage. If it moves first and does not KO, the
   slower survivor still loses its action, and the Exploder's side gets a free
   switch after the trade. A resisted boom can therefore be a route converter
   when chip plus free entry gives the next owner exact range.
3. Machamp is a route piece, not just the current damage button. Its Cross Chop
   and Rock Slide pressure are excellent, but its low Speed and imperfect
   accuracy make preservation matter once a lower-value sack or cash-out gives
   the same endgame with less risk.
4. A sleeping target that is forced to leave is a setup/Substitute branch, not
   just a Cross Chop target. If the expected receiver is Zapdos or Jolteon,
   rank setup behind Substitute before default damage.
