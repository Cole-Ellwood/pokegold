## Reusable Lessons

1. Do not count a sleeping wall as a free Spin setup until the opposing
   spinner branch is priced. If the active move punishes Starmie or Forretress
   entering, keep that pressure above a delayed spinner handoff.
2. Do not spend the spinner's status budget just to clear hazards when an
   already-statused or status-immune teammate can absorb the support move and
   preserve the spinner for the later reset.
3. Cloyster's Spikes job can re-open on Skarmory Rest or a forced passive
   turn. A safe Snorlax hit is acceptable, but it is not always the
   route-converting move.
4. Nidoking's Earthquake/Lovely Kiss branch map improved after the earlier
   packets, but I still sometimes under-priced Earthquake into Starmie/Raikou
   branches after naming them.
