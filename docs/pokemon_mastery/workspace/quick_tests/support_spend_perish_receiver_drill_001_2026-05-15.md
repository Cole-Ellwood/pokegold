# Support Spend / Perish Receiver Drill 001 - 2026-05-15

Purpose: convert `side_known_transfer_027` support-sack and Perish-route misses
into compact nonblind regression checks. This is not fresh progress proof.

## Case 1 - Dying Cloyster With Unfinished Spikes

State: Cloyster is in against +1 Machamp. Cloyster will likely die to Cross
Chop, but Spikes are not on the opponent's side.

Answer: if Cloyster cannot safely remove Machamp with a confirmed legal move,
Spikes can be the route-converting spend before death. Do not switch only to
preserve a support piece whose key job is still unfinished.

Grade: pass.

## Case 2 - Low Cloyster With Cash-Out Threat

State: boosted Snorlax faces Cloyster at 20%; Cloyster has already laid Spikes
and can still Explode before Snorlax moves.

Answer: now guard with Gengar or another named absorber unless Snorlax's attack
is exact removal before Explosion. The support job is complete, so cash-out
protection becomes top.

Grade: pass.

## Case 3 - Perish Before Trap

State: Gengar faces Umbreon. Mean Look is not yet active, but Perish Song can
force Umbreon to leave and may catch the incoming receiver next turn.

Answer: Perish Song is allowed only if the expected receiver, next-turn Mean
Look, and Gengar exit plan are named. Otherwise it is not stable progress.

Grade: pass.

## Case 4 - Own Perish Count Low

State: Gengar has perish1, the opponent's Golem is trapped but not perished,
and Golem can Rapid Spin, Earthquake, or Explode.

Answer: exit if possible, or use Destiny Bond when staying must cover the
immediate action. Do not tunnel Ice Punch damage unless it is exact removal and
Gengar survives the count.

Grade: pass.

Result: 4/4. Regression only.
