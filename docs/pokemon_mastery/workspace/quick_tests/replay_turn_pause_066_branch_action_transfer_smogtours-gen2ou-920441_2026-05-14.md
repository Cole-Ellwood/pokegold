## Error Classes Found

- Branch-action transfer remains weak. Turns 4 and 13 are the clearest misses:
  named or available support branches were not met by the action that beat them.
- Sleep and preservation improved in one spot. Turn 12 correctly switched the
  sleeping Raikou out and saved it while Skarmory met Snorlax.
- Hazard-loop timing remains unstable. Turn 7 correctly set Spikes through the
  Gengar branch, but turn 8 missed Protect as the clean scout into that same
  branch.
- Hidden role pricing needs work. Golem as Rapid Spin support was missed because
  I imported the usual Explosion/Rock Slide pressure role too quickly.

## Reusable Lesson

The branch-action card helped only partially. It forced a better turn 7 answer,
but not the critical turn 4 or turn 13 answers. The missing subskill is role
interrogation: before choosing the branch-beating action, ask whether the
opponent's current Pokemon may be a support role rather than the standard
attacker, and whether Protect/scout is the action that best prices the branch.
