## Scenarios

1. Our Snorlax lead with Lovely Kiss faces Exeggutor lead, and we have
   RestTalk Raikou. Top: Raikou absorber if Exeggutor's item/status package is
   more dangerous than our slower sleep script.
2. After Raikou catches Exeggutor Thief, Exeggutor remains active but Snorlax
   is the obvious special-wall handoff. Top: Thunder, not Hidden Power into the
   visible Exeggutor.
3. Electric lead faces Cloyster lead with no Ground revealed. Top: Thunder or
   phaze pressure; if the expected Snorlax/Ground enters, the next turn must
   solve that owner rather than repeating Electric damage by habit.
4. Exeggutor reveals Thief into an item absorber and stays in. Top: classify
   whether the job is done; if done, rank the receiver punish before active
   damage into Exeggutor.
5. Jynx or Nidoking lead threatens faster sleep into Snorlax. Top: name the
   sleep absorber or faster pressure route before clicking a slower status
   script.
6. Lead support job fails or misses. Top: re-solve from the public result; do
   not keep following the planned absorber route if the item/status transaction
   never happened.

## Reusable Rule

Openings are transactions. Before ranked candidates, answer:

`their lead job -> my absorber or converter -> their counter-handoff -> my
branch punish`.
