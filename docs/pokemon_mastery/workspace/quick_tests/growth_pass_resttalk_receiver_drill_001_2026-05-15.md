# Growth Pass / RestTalk Receiver Drill 001 - 2026-05-15

Purpose: convert the `side_known_transfer_026` turn-6 and turn-25 misses into
four compact nonblind regression checks. This is not fresh progress proof.

## Case 1 - Lead Jynx After Sleep

State: RestTalk Raikou has just absorbed Lovely Kiss from lead Jynx. Jynx has
Ice Beam and Lovely Kiss revealed; Thief is not revealed.

Answer: switch to a lower-value item sack or itemless pivot if available before
default Sleep Talk. Thief is a strong-prior part of the lead Jynx package after
sleep lands. If no item sack exists, Sleep Talk is allowed only with the item
loss named as the cost.

Grade: pass.

## Case 2 - Growth Espeon Versus RestTalk Snorlax

State: +2 Espeon at 53% faces sleeping Snorlax at 58%. Snorlax has Rest, Sleep
Talk, and Earthquake revealed. Normal STAB is not revealed.

Answer: Baton Pass to the receiver that survives Earthquake and strong-prior
Normal STAB while keeping the boost useful. Do not Psychic unless damage KOs
before Sleep Talk or the Normal STAB roll cannot remove Espeon.

Grade: pass.

## Case 3 - Boosted Passer Versus Phazer Branch

State: +1 Espeon faces Zapdos at 64%; Zapdos has Thunderbolt and Whirlwind
revealed, Spikes are on Zapdos's side, and sleeping Snorlax can enter.

Answer: compare Psychic into Zapdos against Growth/Baton Pass by naming the
Snorlax and phaze branches. If Snorlax is expected, Growth or pass can be top;
if Zapdos stays, Psychic must be high because Whirlwind denies idle boosting.

Grade: pass.

## Case 4 - Sleeping Wall Handoff To Spinner

State: Spikes are on the opponent's side, their Snorlax is sleeping, and their
spinner is not revealed but the team has not shown hazard control.

Answer: before using the sleeping wall as setup bait, price the spinner/phazer
handoff class. A move that punishes the handoff, keeps Spikes, or forces the
spinner low can beat generic setup.

Grade: pass.

Result: 4/4. Regression only.
