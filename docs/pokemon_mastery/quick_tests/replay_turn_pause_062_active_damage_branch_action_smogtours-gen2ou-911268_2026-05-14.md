## Error Classes Found

- Branch-action miss: I correctly named likely Snorlax absorber switches on
  turns 5 and 9, but chose the active move instead of the counter-switch.
- Utility timing miss: Golem's best job on turn 6 was Rapid Spin before taking

- Support preservation: Forretress was preserved out of Zapdos instead of
  trying to force Spikes immediately.

## Reusable Lesson

Active-damage persistence is not permission to ignore the branch you just
named. If the opponent's best branch is a switch that changes the board, choose
the counter-switch or utility move when it gains more than one extra hit.
