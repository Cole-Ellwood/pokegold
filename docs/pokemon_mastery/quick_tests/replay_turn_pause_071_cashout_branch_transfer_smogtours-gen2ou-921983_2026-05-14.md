## Policy Extraction

Trigger:
  A Baton Pass chain has named a receiver that blanks or punishes the active
  damage move.

Default:
  Choose the phaze, counter-switch, sacrifice, or coverage action that beats the
  receiver. If Zapdos has Whirlwind and the receiver is Marowak, Whirlwind is
  the route action.

Exceptions:
  Keep attacking only when the current target cannot pass safely, the receiver
  still takes route-changing damage, or the phazer loses to the ordinary attack
  before it can act.

Worst branch:
  You correctly say "Baton Pass to Marowak," click Electric damage anyway, and
  give the opponent the boosted Marowak board for free.
