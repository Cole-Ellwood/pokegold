## Error Classes Found

- Branch-action after naming remains the largest miss class. Turns 3 and 10
  both name the opponent's switch but choose the wrong action into it.
- Preservation transfer is partial. Turn 2 correctly spent Forretress on the
  spinner, and turn 13 correctly used Reflect before Explosion; turn 12 missed
  Cloyster as the lower-value absorber handoff.
- Hazard conversion improved but is not stable. Turn 7 missed Whirlwind as a
  Spikes converter after Tentacruel was gone; turn 8 then overcorrected toward
  phazing when direct Thunderbolt pressure met the Raikou branch.
- Hidden set reveals must force a re-solve. Sand Attack Skarmory, Reflect
  Cloyster, Defense Curl Snorlax, and Curse Tyranitar were all local set
  information that changed the route.

## Reusable Lesson

A route job is not just preserve or spend. It can be "spend Forretress to
remove the spinner," "use Reflect before spending Cloyster," "switch the
lower-value absorber into CurseLax," or "choose the coverage move that actually
beats the switch you named." The current failure is not recognizing the branch;
it is still selecting a move that does not cash in the recognition.
