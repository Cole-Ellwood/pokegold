## Reusable Lesson

The branch-beating action is not just a move category; it is whatever changes
the next receiver map. In this replay, the right action was sometimes Reflect,
Spikes, Roar, Curse, Toxic, Earthquake, a hard switch, or Lovely Kiss. Before
choosing, ask which one makes the opponent's next receiver worse or makes their
current support role fail.
