## Retrieval Rule

Before fresh answer freeze, use only `live_core.md`, the prompt, and at most
one tiny heuristic card. After scoring, use this map to open the old card,
review, quick test, ledger row, or mechanics fixture that explains the miss.

## Non-Deletion Policy

The long policy cards, cookbook, source ledger, paused atlas, worked examples,
reviews, quick tests, external research returns, and mechanics docs remain the
evidence archive. They are demoted from live decision context, not removed.
