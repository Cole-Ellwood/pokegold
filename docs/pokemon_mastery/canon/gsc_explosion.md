# GSC Canon: Explosion And One-Time Trades

Use when Explosion, Self-Destruct, Destiny Bond, or a forced sacrifice may
convert the route or spend a unique piece.

## Route Rules

- Explosion and Self-Destruct are one-time route pieces. They are correct when
  the trade removes the blocker, opens the cleaner, denies recovery/setup, or
  wins the board state now.
- Do not cash out into the wrong absorber. Ghosts are immune to Normal-type
  Explosion/Self-Destruct in vanilla GSC, and resists can turn a cash-out into
  a lost piece.
- A low-HP support Pokemon is not automatically disposable. Preserve it if its
  remaining job is still unique: Spikes, Spin, sleep absorption, phazing,
  screen, Perish, or emergency revenge.
- Conversely, preserving a piece with no remaining route job can be worse than
  cashing out into the converter.
- Explosion routes must name the next owner after the trade. If the opponent's
  next entry owns the board, the cash-out may be only cosmetic.
- For romhack advice, verify any local type, ability, item, or move changes
  before treating vanilla Explosion immunity/resistance as final.

## Move-Choice Checks

1. What blocker does the cash-out remove?
2. What exact route opens after both Pokemon leave the board?
3. What absorber or Ghost branch punishes the cash-out?
4. Does the exploding piece still have a unique future job?
