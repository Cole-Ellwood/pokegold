# GSC Canon: Spikes, Rapid Spin, And Spinblock

Use when the turn is about setting Spikes, preserving them, spinning, blocking
Spin, or converting hazard pressure into a route.

## Route Rules

- Vanilla GSC has one layer of Spikes. It pressures grounded switch-ins and
  matters because GSC games often revolve around repeated forced entries.
- Spikes is progress only if the route preserves or converts it. A layer that
  is immediately spun away or ignored by the actual route is often a script.
- Rapid Spin is not just damage removal. It can reset the opponent's whole
  hazard route, which makes spin denial a branch-punish question.
- Ghosts block Rapid Spin. Name the spinner, the Ghost, and the cost of
  bringing the Ghost in before calling the block correct.
- Flying-types are not hit by Spikes. Do not overstate hazard pressure on
  Zapdos, Skarmory, Dragonite, or other airborne routes.
- In the romhack, check `../romhack_deltas/spikes_and_rapid_spin.md`; local
  Spikes behavior diverges from vanilla GSC.

## Move-Choice Checks

1. If I click Spikes, what prevents Spin or immediate route reset?
2. If I click Rapid Spin, what Ghost or punish branch enters?
3. If I switch to the Ghost, what damage/status/setup does it take?
4. Does attacking or cashing out convert faster than hazard maintenance?
