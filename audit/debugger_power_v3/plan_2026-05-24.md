# Debugger Power V3 Plan

## Objective

Make the unified debugger materially more capable than V2 by turning proof
cards into an audited proof campaign runner.

V2 can execute one safe proof command for one routed question. V3 should execute
many concrete proof commands in one pass, classify every known route command,
dedupe repeated commands, and distinguish expected discrepancies from debugger
failures.

## Selected Slice

Implement proof-campaign autopilot:

- `python -m tools.debugger prove --suite audit/debugger_power_v3/questions.jsonl --all-routes --execute`
- classify all `NEXT_STEP_ROWS` commands as executed, blocked by placeholder,
  rejected as unsafe, or classified-only;
- execute the curated concrete proof suite once per unique command;
- support expected nonzero proof commands as `discrepancy_found`;
- keep all outputs under `.local/tmp` through the audit wrapper.

This is the highest-leverage slice because it multiplies the existing V2
`prove` surface instead of adding more one-off routes.

## Acceptance Contract

`python tools/audit/check_debugger_power_v3.py` must prove:

- V2 still passes;
- godmode benchmark remains 29/29 with `investigate_run_count=0`;
- all 35 route rows are classified;
- at least 20 symptom classes receive an executed or blocked disposition;
- at least 18 unique safe commands execute;
- at least one expected nonzero proof is classified as `discrepancy_found`;
- at least 10 placeholder or unsafe commands have concrete blocked reasons;
- no unsafe command is executed;
- the V3 pass completes under 90 seconds in the current dirty workspace.

## Rejected V3 Slices

Placeholder input resolution is valuable, especially for Boss AI scenario and
save-backed commands, but it should follow this slice because campaign results
will show exactly which placeholders matter most.

Paraphrase-resistant routing is also useful, but extra synonyms or fuzzy
matching are not a 10x capability jump unless paired with executable proof.

## Guardrails

Do not touch ROM source, generated docs, ROM outputs, symbol files, or dirty Boss
AI trace artifacts. V3 lives in `tools/*` and `audit/*`.
