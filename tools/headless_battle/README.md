# Headless Battle Simulator

`tools.headless_battle` is the first text/JSON no-GUI battle-state path. It is intentionally narrow: selected move-vs-move turns, selected BP=0 single-stat stage moves, selected Dragon Dance/Calm Mind/Quiver Dance setup moves, selected Recover/Softboiled/Milk Drink self-heal moves, selected PoisonPowder/Poison Gas/Toxic poison-status moves, selected Thunder Wave/Stun Spore/Glare paralysis-status moves, selected EFFECT_BURN_HIT/EFFECT_POISON_HIT/EFFECT_PARALYZE_HIT damaging status secondaries, paralyzed speed/full-paralysis checks including Fighting/Electric type-passive paralysis modifiers, repeated action maps with a max-turn cap, `auto_replace_or` for simple KO/send-out loops, ROM-mirrored wild random move choice, selected switch actions, caller-supplied replacement after KO, explicit enemy `auto_replace` choice after KO, explicit active Potion/Super Potion/Hyper Potion/Max Potion/Full Restore actions, explicit stats/species/stat-stage state, PP decrement for selected moves, supported after-hit item HP effects, modified-speed turn order and equal-speed tie RNG, basic critical-hit checks, basic move accuracy hit/miss checks, damage variation with fixed/sample/exhaustive RNG, selected damaging status secondary chance RNG, initial poison/burn/toxic residual damage after selected moves and supported item actions, selected-action `turns[]` progression with HP/RNG carryover, pre-variation HP damage through the existing damage oracle, post-score Boss AI selector replay, and executable post-score Boss AI selector turns when the scenario supplies final score bytes.

Run:

```powershell
python -m tools.headless_battle --template
python -m tools.headless_battle --scenario .local\tmp\headless_turn.json
python -m tools.headless_battle --scenario .local\tmp\headless_turn.json --json
python tools\audit\check_headless_battle_simulator.py
```

Proof boundary:

- `damage_core_pre_variation` is byte-proven through the existing `tools.damage_debugger.oracle.predict_damage` and `tools.damage_debugger.clobber_smoke` gate.
- `basic_critical_hit_rng` mirrors `BattleCommand_Critical` for the NormalHit command order: critical check first, damage variation second, accuracy third.
- `damage_variation_rng_branching` mirrors `BattleCommand_DamageVariation`: 0/1 damage skips variation; otherwise accepted multipliers are 217..255 after the ROM's rotate-right rejection loop.
- `basic_move_accuracy_rng` mirrors the basic `BattleCommand_CheckHit` raw-byte threshold for move accuracy, always-hit moves, and Thunder in rain.
- `basic_status_residual` mirrors `ResidualDamage` for initial poison, burn, and toxic residual after a selected move when both active Pokemon remain alive.
- `basic_pp_decrement` mirrors the supported `BattleCommand_DoTurn` PP decrement: selected and executable-selector moves lose 1 PP before their supported effect path, and zero-PP selected moves are rejected until Struggle is implemented.
- `supported_after_hit_item_effects` mirrors Rocky Helmet contact recoil, Shell Bell healing, and Life Orb recoil after successful damaging hits. The isolated handler effects are byte-proven by `tools.damage_debugger.clobber_smoke`; the headless simulator applies the same HP fractions and item order for this subset.
- `explicit_active_hp_restore_items` mirrors the source HP amounts, max-HP caps, Full Restore's supported status/toxic cure, turn consumption, and actor residual after an explicit active Potion/Super Potion/Hyper Potion/Max Potion/Full Restore action. This is a sandbox scenario action: ordinary trainer-battle Pack availability, automatic trainer item dispatch, bag inventory accounting, bench targeting, revives, PP restore, X items, and item text/scripts remain out of scope.
- `selected_turn_order_priority_speed` is source-mirrored for priority, unequal modified speed, and non-link equal-speed ties.
- `explicit_stat_stage_state` supports caller-supplied active stat stages for attack/defense/speed/sp_attack/sp_defense in the user-facing -6..+6 range. Damage stats and turn-order speed use `data/battle/stat_multipliers.asm`; critical-hit stat handling follows `CheckDamageStatsCritical`; switch/replacement resets stages. Accuracy/evasion stages, stage-changing moves outside the selected single-stat BP=0 subset, Baton Pass/Psych Up, badge boosts, status speed, and passive stat/speed modifiers remain out of scope.
- `selected_stat_stage_only_moves` supports selected BP=0 single-stat attack/defense/speed/sp_attack/sp_defense stage moves. Self-raising moves mutate the actor; opponent-lowering moves run the basic accuracy check first; caps/floors report no effect. Accuracy/evasion stage moves, damaging secondary stat effects outside selected burn/poison/paralysis status secondaries, multi-stat chains outside the selected setup subset, Substitute/Mist blockers, and text/animation side effects remain out of scope.
- `selected_multi_stat_setup_moves` supports Dragon Dance, Calm Mind, and Quiver Dance. These moves consume PP, mutate their chained supported stat stages, report cap/no-effect through the shared stat-stage machinery, and influence later damage and turn order. Dragon Dance mirrors `bestattackup` using the simulator-supported current attack/sp_attack values; burn/passive attack modifiers, Curse, Psych Up, Baton Pass, Substitute/Mist blockers, and text/animation side effects remain out of scope.
- `selected_damaging_status_secondaries` supports selected EFFECT_BURN_HIT, EFFECT_POISON_HIT, and EFFECT_PARALYZE_HIT damaging moves. These consume secondary effect-chance RNG after the successful hit path, apply burn/poison/paralysis after damage when source no-effect checks pass, and preserve later residual/speed effects. Thunder, Flame Wheel, Sacred Fire, poison multi-hit, freeze/confusion/stat-down secondaries, Substitute/Safeguard, held status item consumption, and text/animation side effects remain out of scope.
- `selected_self_heal_moves` supports Recover, Softboiled, and Milk Drink. These moves consume PP, restore half max HP with a max-HP cap, report full-HP no-effect cases, and then run supported post-action residual. Rest uses the same source command but adds full HP restoration, sleep/status handling, and stat recalculation, so Rest remains out of scope.
- `selected_poison_status_moves` supports PoisonPowder, Poison Gas, and Toxic. These moves consume PP, run the basic accuracy check, apply poison/toxic to healthy non-Poison non-immune targets, initialize `toxic_count` at 0, and preserve later residual ticks. Poison-type targets, type immunity, and already-statused targets report no effect. Safeguard, Substitute, held status-healing item consumption, and damaging poison secondary effects outside the selected secondary subset remain out of scope.
- `selected_paralysis_status_moves` supports Thunder Wave, Stun Spore, and Glare. These moves consume PP only after the full-paralysis gate lets the actor move, run the basic accuracy check, apply paralysis to healthy non-immune targets, reduce future turn-order speed through `ApplyPrzEffectOnSpeed` including the Fighting/Electric type-passive paralysis modifiers, and branch full-paralysis action denial with `TypePassive_GetUserParalysisFailThreshold_Far` thresholds. Type immunity and already-statused targets report no effect. Safeguard, Substitute, held prevent/cure items, non-paralyzed Electric speed passives, and damaging paralysis secondary effects outside the selected secondary subset remain out of scope.
- `multi_turn_selected_action_progression` carries active HP/RNG state across a caller-supplied `turns[]` list and stops early on KO.
- `repeat_plan_auto_replace_or` lets a scenario use `repeat: {"max_turns": N, "actions": ...}` or top-level `max_turns` to reuse one action map until battle-over or the cap. `auto_replace_or` uses a normal fallback action while the side is alive and explicit `auto_replace` when fainted.
- `selected_switch_and_replacement` supports caller-selected `switch` actions from `bench[]` and caller-supplied `replace` actions after a KO. Both entry paths reset stat stages.
- `auto_replacement_choice_basic_type_chart` supports explicit enemy `auto_replace` actions after KO. It mirrors the source-shaped trainer replacement preference using `data/types/type_matchups.asm`: prefer a living bench Pokemon with a super-effective move, otherwise avoid a living bench Pokemon hit super-effectively by the player's public types, otherwise use low-three-bit random rejection. Matchup overlays from Dragon's Majesty, Air Balloon, Foresight/identified state, and switch-in effects remain out of scope.
- `boss_ai_selector_from_post_score_bytes` reuses `select_from_score_bytes` for already-known final score bytes.
- `boss_ai_selector_move_execution` branches fixed/sample/exhaustive selector RNG over already-known final score bytes, records the chosen slot, validates the selected move id when available, and then executes the selected move through the same turn-order and damage path.
- `wild_random_move_choice` mirrors the four-slot wild enemy random move loop for non-empty, nonzero-PP moves. Fixed/sample modes consume bytes until a legal slot appears; exhaustive mode branches one outcome per legal slot.

Out of scope until separate implementation and proof: trainer/Boss automatic action choice without caller-supplied final Boss AI score bytes, automatic trainer item turns, player trainer-battle Pack availability, item inventory accounting, implicit replacement without an `auto_replace` action, forced switch prompts, Pursuit-on-switch, Spikes/switch-in effects, RNG-consuming mechanics outside speed ties/full paralysis/Boss AI selector choice/wild random move choice/auto-replace fallback/critical hits/accuracy/damage variation/selected damaging status secondary chance, accuracy/evasion stat modifiers and stage moves, damaging secondary stat effects outside selected burn/poison/paralysis status secondaries, multi-stat chains outside Dragon Dance/Calm Mind/Quiver Dance, BrightPowder, Protect, Fly/Dig, Lock-On, X Accuracy, Baton Pass/Psych Up, Substitute/Mist blockers, badge boosts, passive stat/speed/accuracy bonuses outside the listed paralysis path, link-battle turn-order inversion, Quick Claw/Choice Scarf turn-order effects, sleep, freeze, burn application outside selected damaging burn secondaries, Safeguard/Substitute, held status prevent/cure item consumption, non-paralyzed Electric speed passives, volatile effects, weather/time healing, Rest, drain moves, Heal Bell, unsupported item recovery/cures, Air Balloon pop, Substitute-blocked contact, Focus Punch break, after-hit text/script effects outside supported HP mutations, Struggle, PP Up bit packing, Mimic/Transform PP routing, live Boss AI scoring, Boss AI switch candidate/confidence generation, scripts, text, animations, EXP, and party writes.
